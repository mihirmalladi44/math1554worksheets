    \({\textbf{Worksheet Exercises}}\text{:}\)

    1. Recall the following theorem from lectures:
    🔗
    \({\mathbf{Theorem}}\text{:}\)
    🔗
    If \(T_A : \mathbb{R}^n \mapsto \mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) and \(S\) is some parallelogram in \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) then
    🔗
    \begin{equation*} \operatorname{volume}(T_A(S)) = |\textup{det} (A)| \cdot \operatorname{volume}(S) \end{equation*}
    where \(T_A(\vec{x}) = A\vec{x}\text{.}\)
    🔗
    \(\)
    🔗
    \(\)
    🔗
    \(S\) is the parallelogram determined by \(\vec{p_1} = \begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ 4 \end{pmatrix}\text{,}\) and \(\vec{p_2} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}\text{.}\)
    🔗
    1. What is the area of \(S\text{?}\)
      🔗
      🔗
    2. If \(A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \amp -1 \\ 1 \amp 1 \end{pmatrix}\text{,}\) what is the area of the image of \(R\) under the map \(\vec{x} \mapsto A\vec{x}\text{?}\)
